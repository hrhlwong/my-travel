Japan is an island nation in East Asia, located in the Pacific Ocean. It consists of four major islands—Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku, and Kyushu—along with thousands of smaller ones. The country stretches over 1,500 miles and is known for its mountainous terrain, active volcanoes, and frequent seismic activity

With a population of over 123 million, Japan is the 11th most populous country in the world. Its capital, Tokyo, is a global hub of technology, fashion, and culture.
